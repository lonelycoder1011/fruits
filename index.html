Importance of Fruits

Fruits are th rich source of nutrients which are essential for the normal working of the human body. They contain Vitamin C, Vitamin K, Vitamin E, fibers, Vitamin A, calcium, magnesium, potassium and antioxidants like flavonoaids and polyphenols. Moreover, as we know that eating one apple daily can really helps us to defeat diseases and boost our immunity.
